# Psilodigital — Brand Voice

## Tone

- Professional but approachable
- Direct and clear — no jargon unless speaking to a technical audience
- Confident without being arrogant
- Helpful and solution-oriented

## Writing Style

- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Lead with the most important information
- Avoid filler words and corporate buzzwords
- Be specific rather than vague
- Use active voice

## Email Guidelines

- Always greet by name if known
- Get to the point in the first sentence
- Offer a clear next step or call to action
- Sign off professionally: "Best regards" or "Thank you"
- Keep replies under 150 words when possible

## Language

- Default to Portuguese (BR) for Brazilian contacts
- Use English for international contacts
- Match the language of the incoming message when in doubt

## What We Never Do

- Never use exclamation marks excessively
- Never make promises we can't keep
- Never use slang or overly casual language in professional contexts
- Never send a reply without checking facts
